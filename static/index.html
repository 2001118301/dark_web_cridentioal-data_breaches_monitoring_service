⚠️ Why Credential Security Matters

Identity Theft Risk

Compromised credentials are the #1 cause of identity theft. Hackers use leaked emails and passwords to access your banking, social media, and personal accounts.

Credential Stuffing

Attackers use automated tools to try your leaked password across thousands of other websites. If you reuse passwords, one leak compromises everything.

Permanent Exposure

Once data is on the dark web, it stays there forever. Continuous monitoring is the only way to know when your information resurfaces in new breaches.
